3 – How to Earn Points

3.1 – GENERAL TERMS

Members can earn CLUB Reward Points ("Reward Points") by making a qualifying purchase of Eau Thermale Avene products ("Qualifying Purchase" means all Eau Thermale Avene products distributed in Singapore by Color Play Enterprise Pte Ltd excluding promotions such as bundle packs, kits and others special promotions) at Participating Stores. Once registered as a CLUB Member (see above), you must follow the following process to register new purchases in your application:

- Step 1: Scan via the Application the EAN barcode of the Eau Thermale Avene product.

- Step 2: Manually input the Unique Code available on Product Sticker.

- Step 3: Submit all information corresponding to the purchased product such as date of purchase, Retailer type, area of purchase and store of purchase location.

Once submitted, your points will be credited to your account around 2 (two) working days after the date of purchase. Please note that The CLUB reserves the right to ask Members for their receipt at any time.

3.2 – EARNING POINTS, POINT CALCULATION AND POINTS EXPIRY

All Members will earn points when they: (i) purchase qualifying products ("Qualifying Products") at Participating Stores (i.e., make a Qualifying Purchase), AND (ii) provide visible EAN barcode scanned, unique sticker code as well as all information required, to the customer service via the mobile application.

They will earn ten (10) points for every one dollar (SGD 1) spent. In case of cents: one (1) point for every ten cents (SGD 0.10) spent. For instance, if a Member spends $9.50, he/she will receive 95 points and if a Member spends $9.60, he/she will receive 96 points. For spend amounts that include a cent value of $0.04 or less, points earned will be rounded down to the nearest number. For instance, if a Member spends $9.84, he/she will receive 98 points. For spend amounts that include a cent value of $0.05 or more, points earned will be rounded up to the nearest number. For instance, if a Member spends $9.86, he/she will receive 99 points or if a Member spends $9.85, he/she will receive 99 points.

Points corresponding to the purchase of one (1) Eau Thermale Avene product will expire 365 (three hundred sixty five) days after the date of submission by the Member of the purchase in the CLUB Application. Member may check expiring points using the Application.

3.3 - ACCRUAL AND CONFIRMATION OF POINTS EARNED:

Reward Points will be credited to your account around 2 (two) working days following the time of the corresponding submission on the Application of the Qualifying Purchase.

3.4 - EXCLUSIONS

Members earn Reward Points only on the purchase of Qualifying Products at Participating Stores. Products deemed Qualifying Products are in the sole discretion of Eau Thermale Avene Laboratories, Pierre Fabre Dermo Cosmetique Singapore Pte Ltd, and Color Play Enterprise Pte Ltd. The following products, items, charges, and purchases are SPECIFICALLY EXCLUDED as Qualifying Products and/or Qualifying Purchases: promotional items such as bundle packs, kits and other promotions in kind; returned items; department store and other retailer purchases than the ones mentioned above; purchases made prior to the launch of the CLUB; shipping and handling charges. Reward Points will be earned only on the actual listed price of an item at the time of purchase, and not on any other original, previous, suggested retail, or manufacturer's suggested price.

The following purchases are also SPECIFICALLY UNELIGIBLE for the collection of points: (i) purchases made by a Member prior to joining the CLUB; and (ii) purchases made without first identifying and confirming your Account information. Purchases made outside Singapore are also uneligible for Singapore CLUB.

3.5 - RETURNS

Reward Points earned pursuant to a Qualifying Purchase that correspond to a subsequent return, refund, returned check due to non-sufficient funds, or other credit will be deducted from the Member's account in an amount equal to the Reward Points earned for the original transaction.

3.6 - BONUS REWARD POINTS

After initial registration as a CLUB Member and confirmation of the member account, the new Member will have the possibility to earn for a one & only time 50 (fifty) bonus points upon 100% (hundred per cent) completion of its profile.

4 – How to Redeem Reward Points

4.1 – GENERAL

Reward Points earned by a Member may be redeemed only via the CLUB Mobile application for the items available in the “Rewards Catalogue” available in the Menu of the application. All redeemed items will have to be collected only at the collection point indicated in the redemption confirmation message sent to the Member once the redemption will be submitted by the Member.

4.2 – REDEMPTION TERMS AND CONDITIONS

a. Members will have the possibility to redeem free items via the CLUB Application only once they will have accumulated a minimum level of “Reward Points”.

b. Levels of redemption will be available to the CLUB Member with dedicated redemption catalogues for each of these levels.

c. A Member can redeem several items at the same time as long as the corresponding Reward Points are available in its account.

d. Upon submission of its redemption order, the Member will receive a confirmation of its redemption by email with a “Redemption Number” and all the relevant information regarding the collection.

e. The redeemed “Reward Points” will then be deducted from the Member Account balance.

f. The redeemed items will be available immediately at the collection point for pick-up by the Member. All items for redemption are available while stocks last.

g. To collect the redeemed items at the location of redemption, the Member will have to provide the “Redemption Number” indicated on the email confirmation he would have received after submission of its redemption order.

h. Redeemed items will be valid for a period of 30 (thirty) days from submission date of redemption order. After 30 (thirty) days and if the redeemed items have not been collected, the Member will receive a reminder notification by email and will be granted 15 (fifteen) additional days to collect the items. After this total of 45 (forty-five) days upon submission of redemption order and in the case the Member has not collected the items, the points will be definitely considered as lost.

i. Levels of redemption can be accessed in the App via the “Rewards Catalogue” page (and may be subject to change).
