General Questions

What is Avène club?

Avène club reward program is how Avène showing our appreciation to thank loyal customers for purchasing Avène products in Singapore. Purchase only applicable to Watsons and Guardian store.

As a valued Avène Club member, you can get exclusive information on promotions, events and information. Points accumulated can be used to redeem Avène product.

How do I update my personal particulars?

Kindly use the Avène Club mobile app to update your personal particulars at “Update Profile” page. Keeping your information updated is important to receiving information on new launches and promotions!

Points and Membership

How does the Avène Club Program work?

You can join Avène Club by purchasing at least one Avène item from Watson or Guardians. After which you can download the Avène app and scan the barcode to collect points. For every $0.10 cents you spend, you will earn 1 point. Points earned can be used to redeem attractive rewards from Avène.

How long does it take for the Avène Points to be updated in your system?

It will take 48 hours for Avène app to update the points to your account.

How do I check the Avène Club Points?

You can check their point summary by logging on the Avène mobile app that is available in app Store.

Will my Avène Club points be expired?

Yes, Avène Club points will be expired in 1 year time.

Is there an annual membership fee?

It is free. However, members need to purchase at least 1 item yearly to retain the membership. <To be advice from Avène>

Is the membership transferable?

Memberships are non-transferable and cannot be shared with family members, friends or others.

Redemption

What should I do if I have problems with scanning of barcode and capturing of receipts?

If you are facing difficulties with scanning of barcode and capturing of receipts, you may contact our Customer Service Line at +65 64534955 or email to admin@colorplay.com.sg

If I have receipts that were given to me, can I use it to earn Avène Points?

Avène Points can only be awarded for your own purchases. If any member is found using receipts that were not their own, Avène reserve the rights to terminate their membership and take further action to investigate.

I have placed a redemption order via Avène mobile app, when will the Avène products be ready for collection?

After you have placed a redemption order, it will be ready for collection 2 days after your redemption order is received. Please collect your redemption within 30 business days, or your redemption reference code will no longer be valid.
